    Cybersecurity: Free 2 Hour SMB Review 

    BI Goals SMB
    Charge: Free

    An engagement to evaluate how cybersecurity tools and processes can help you manage your IT assets, and streamline your cybersecurity and security operations, with the unique challenges of running a small and medium sized businesses (SMB)


    BI Goals will evaluate your SMB business needs and determine which cybersecurity processes and tools can help you manage your workstations, servers, network, backup strategies, browser protection, email, training, and security policies.

    Pre-Meeting

    1. Answer the questions in the Excel spreadsheet. If you have a quick question, you can call for clarifcation. If you have many questions, please batch them up in an email and I will respond as quickly as possible, either by email or by phone.

    It will be your responsibility to download the 2 Hour Cybersecurity Review Excel spreadsheet and then fill it out as completely as possible.

    The Excel spreadsheet can be found here.

    Since this is mostly a DIY process, we together can only be successful if you put in the time to answer most of the questions in the Review spreadsheet.

    Once you have answered the questions, please send us the Excel spreadsheet also.

    If you do not have Excel installed on any computer, Google Docs can help here.

    Why DIY? To save you the expense of having a consultant come to your business and conduct there reviews and possible remediations in person. Also it provides you a layer of security protection to not have anybody on site.

    3. Remind attendees in advance of the meeting, so that we get a consense of your stakeholder employees. A day or two notice might be appropriate, also a final reminder hours before incase a critical stakeholder is in another meeting.

    The meeting time will be in Pacific Standard Time (PST).

    Agenda

    We will have an interactive discussion to gain insight into your SMB organization to ensure your business success for today and tomorrow with a stronger cybersecurity position.

    1. Identify your cybersecurity goals
    2. Identify your current cybersecurity process
    3. Quickly review the Excel assessment summary spreadsheet
    4. Discuss security pains and needs

    Deliverables

    1. Identification of the current pains, needs and requirements
    2. Review internal business processes
    3. Identification of your cybersecurity goals and objectives for your organization
    4. Deliver cybersecurity recommendations for improved protection, safety, and reliability
    5. Develop a strategic cybersecurity implementation plan or roadmap

    Assumptions

    • One company included, excludes intercompany, subsidiaries
    • The company resides in the United States or D.C.
    • The company has less than 1500 employees
    • The company has less than 30 external or satellite offices
    • Windows operating system only
    • Customer will perform all the configuration settings, software installation and scripting as needed, this is a guided DIY cybersecurity review
    • This offer does not include scripts, system analysis, fit/gap process, system modifications or integrations but are available if requested for additional fees

    Note

    Please come prepared to the online meeting with supporting information to aid in a mutually successful discussion about your business needs per the above agenda.

    Please download the 2 Hour Cybersecurity Review Excel spreadsheet from your email and have as much as  you can filled in by the meeting. If you have most or all of the data collected and put into the Excel spreadsheet, please forward a copy by email (bigoals365 (at) gmail (dot) com) to us. 

    The assessment review will be conducted remotely via screen sharing and teleconferencing.
